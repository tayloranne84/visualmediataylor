Professor McGehee

Course description:

This course is the introduction to travel and tourism both domestically and abroad. It includes topics such as the history, sociology and psychology of tourism.

Professor McGehee's bio/interests:

Professor McGehee earned her PhD from Virginia Tech and is the head of the Department of Hospitality and Tourism Management. She was honored as an "Outstanding Woman in Travel Research" by Women in Travel and Tourism International.

Overview:

This class has been interesting because it isn't one of the standard classes I take for my major. It's been interesting to learn new things about travel and tourism all over the world.
