Professor Reep

Course description:

This course teaches survey of the art of music encompassing a variety of music styles and historical eras in both the western and non-western world.

Professor Reep's bio/interests:

Professor Reep is the assistant Director of Athletic Bands at Virginia Tech. He earned his Masters of Music in Wind Conducting at James Madison University.

Overview:

I've enjoyed this class because I played the cello for 7 years and it has been nice to find a way to continue enjoying and keeping up with music. It's been interesting learning about different types of music this semester compared to the Theory/Fundamentals class I took last year.
