Professor Faircloth

Course description:

This classes teaches the principles and basic concepts of human interaction and social organization. Basic theories and research methods, socialization, deviance, social inequality, social institutions, population dynamics, and social change.

Professor Faircloth's bio/interests:

Professor Faircloth earned her PhD at Virginia Tech and also serves as co-advisor for Native at VT. She plans to bring the first Intertribal powwow to Tech's campus.

Overview:

This is an online class and I highly recommend it. There aren't any tests or quizzes and it's very flexible when it comes to managing time. I feel like I've learned a lot more about sociology through this class and I'm glad I took it.
